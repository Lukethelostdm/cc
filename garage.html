A place filled with workshops, hobbies and maintenance equipment, a garage is often an assortment of many things that have no set place. By decluttering this space, you will see a variety of benefits that go beyond claiming back the room.
Estimated Time:
Seven to Ten hours

Unlock Extra Space:

Decluttering can reveal unused storage potential, allowing you to better organize seasonal items, sports gear, and tools.

A Functional Workspace:

A decluttered garage provides a dedicated area for DIY projects, hobbies, and repairs, enhancing productivity and enjoyment.

Enhance Your Property's Look:

A tidy garage improves the overall appearance of your property, making it more welcoming and visually appealing from the outside.

Streamline Your Hobbies:

With a well-organized garage, you can easily locate items, saving time and reducing frustration in your daily activities.

Efficient Energy:

Removing clutter can help maintain proper insulation and airflow in your garage, leading to better temperature control and energy efficiency.

Prepared For Emergencies:

A clutter-free garage ensures that emergency supplies and equipment, such as generators and first aid kits, are easily accessible when needed.

Tips and tricks for a tidy garage

Clear the floors

A lot can go on in a garage, having a clean floor makes everything safer and easier.

Step One

Remove The Garbage

All the empty packets, broken tool pieces etc. Once these are gone, the task will feel easier as a whole.

Step two

Like With Like.

Keep everything that belongs together, don't just leave it around.

Step three

Use The Walls.

A lot of items can be wall mounted, doing this will free up a lot of space and make it a lot easier to find items.

Step four

Take It Outside

Where possible, it's always the best idea to take everything outside first. The task will seem less daunting and you'll get a good idea of what you want to keep in the process
